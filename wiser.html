Wiser Penny

Our potential users (people with hardly any experience in personal finance) want a Money-Saving app they can easily understand, that is also trustworthy and rewarding.

My Process

Sketches

I decided to start sketching my first set of wireframes with pen & paper to have more creative freedom from the get go.

Flow Diagram

Once I gathered the essential features the users wanted to be included in the app, I developed a Flow Diagram that reflected their needs.

User Testing

Users want to have a graph-like tracker rather than an arrow. They also want to see the amount of the savings progress.

Prototype

Users want to see the percentage progress of their goal.

Conclusion

User Testing played a key role on this project as I uncovered the specific expectations and needs of the target users. A great example was the feedback from users requesting percentages and graphs to help them track their savings goals. This helped me steered the design towards a more suited layout that better serves the users.
